About me


My passion for drawing started when I was a teenager, eventually I applied for the Animation and Digital Art program in Mexico, however, it was not what I expected since I wanted to study something related to Art and experimental animation.

While doing research I found out that The Czech Republic used to produce over 100 animated films per year. Because of this I started to look for Universities and Colleges in Prague so I could studied what I always wanted. I chose Prague College and there is where my career as an artist started, I learned about art movements and how to create concepts and work on them.

Eventually I decided to go back to Mexico and finish my Animation program there, because I came to the conclusion that the university in Mexico City was better in the technical aspects, so I wanted to combine conceptual knowledge I acquired in Prague with the technical skills I was learning in Mexico City.
